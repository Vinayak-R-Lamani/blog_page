Government Polytechnic , Belagavi

Department Of Collegiate And Technical Education

Government Polytechnic Belgaum started functioning during year 1958. Started with Civil, Electrical, Mechanical Engineering at present there are 6 courses with 420 annual intake plus 25 students with disabilities under the scheme of integration people with disabilities in to the mainstream of Technical and Vocational Education. Government Polytechnic Belgaum has a team of dedicated teachers and supporting staff with whom co-operative efforts made the principal to work efficiently and in a manner needed by the present competitive and challenging situation.
